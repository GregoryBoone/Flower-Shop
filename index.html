Wild Flower Club

Flower Subscriptions

Hey! Are you a flower lover? Silly question - you wouldn’t be here if you weren’t! As all flower-lovers know, flowers make the best gifts, are the sweetest form of self-care, and add that magic little something to any space. That’s why we started the Wild Flower Club! A place for you to get flowers regularly and at a discount.

How It Works

Choose Your Frequency

Pick from weekly, bi-weekly, or every four weeks to have blooms ready for you on the reg.

Choose Your Flowers

Customize your blooms in a bunch of ways - size, paper or vase, color - oh my!

Choose Pickup or Delivery

Pick up on a day and time that is convenient for you or let your blooms arrive straight to your home or office door!

SIGN UP NOW

Q: Is there a minimum commitment required to sign up for a subscription?

A: Nope! We ask that you try it for a few weeks/months and if you don't love it, you can cancel!

Q:​ Am I able to set up a subscription as a gift?

A:​ Yes! Subscriptions make awesome gifts! You can set one up for delivery so the recipient receives their flowers right to their door, or for pick up if you know they are already a regular and pop in frequently.

Q:​ Can I change my selected day of the week?

A: ​Yes! A rescheduling email must be sent to hello@nativepoppy.com 72 hours before your next charge date. *Please note, scheduling changes are accommodated infrequently. Changes every week are not permitted.

Q:​ Can I change my color palette?

A:​ Yes! A color palette request email must be sent to hello@nativepoppy.com 72 hours before your next charge date.

Q:​ Can I skip an order?

A:​ Yes! A skip email must be sent to hello@nativepoppy.com 72 hours before your next charge date.

Q:​ If my subscription is set as a pick up, can I pick up at either shop location?

A:​ No, you will have a home shop where you pick up each time. *Your order can occasionally be relocated with 72 hours notice.

Q:​ What happens if I miss my subscription pick up?

A:​ We notify you by phone of a missed pick up and will hold your flowers in our flower fridge for 48 hours. After 48 hours, your order will be forfeited.

Q:​ What happens if I miss my subscription delivery?

A:​ You or the recipient does not need to be home to receive your flowers. If no one answers, your flowers will be left in a safe, shaded area. A delivery driver will call if we are unable to safely deliver your flowers. If we are unable to deliver your flowers, they will be returned to our shop. You then have the option to pick them up or pay for a redelivery.

Q:​ How do I cancel my subscription?

A: ​A cancellation email must be sent to hello@flowershop.com 72 hours before your next charge date.
